# 📊 Maven Market Sales Analysis

A Power BI dashboard analyzing retail sales performance across the USA, Canada, and Mexico for the period **1997–1998**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Maven Market needed a clear view of brand performance, regional sales distribution, and revenue trends to support data-driven decisions on inventory, marketing, and target-setting.

---

## 📁 Dataset

- **Source:** Maven Market 
- **Period:** January 1997 – December 1998
- **Tables:** Transactions, Products, Customers, Stores, Regions, Calendar

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- **Power BI Desktop** — data modeling, DAX measures, dashboard design
- **Power Query** — data cleaning and transformation

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- KPI cards for current month returns, profit, and orders vs. targets
- Brand-level table with total transactions, profit, profit margin, and return rate
- Geographic map showing sales distribution across North America
- Weekly revenue trend line chart
- Revenue vs. Target gauge chart
- Interactive date slicer (1997–1998) 
- Country filter (USA / Canada / Mexico)

---

## 💡 Recommendations

1. **Investigate King Sunglasses brand-wide** — it consistently underperformed across all three countries (lowest transactions, lowest profit margin ~47.25%), suggesting a structural issue such as pricing, demand, or product quality rather than a regional problem.

2. **Prioritize inventory and marketing for Hermanos** — it was the top-performing brand in both the USA and Mexico, showing strong, consistent demand across regions.

3. **Review high return-rate brands per region** (Shady Lakes Pasta in Canada at 3.94%, King Sunglasses in USA at 2.58%) to identify quality or fulfillment issues driving returns.

4. **Study Mexico's order growth (+28.34%) as a model** — unlike the USA and Canada where profit grew while orders dropped, Mexico saw both profit and order volume increase together, which may indicate effective regional strategy worth replicating.
